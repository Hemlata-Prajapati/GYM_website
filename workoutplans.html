Workout Plans

Achieve your fitness goals with structured workout plans designed for every level.

Weekly Workout Schedule

Day 1 - Upper Body

  • Push-ups - 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Pull-ups - 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Bench Press - 3 sets of 12 reps

Day 2 - Lower Body

  • Squats - 3 sets of 20 reps
  • Deadlifts - 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Lunges - 3 sets of 15 reps per leg

Day 3 - Cardio & Core

  • Running - 30 minutes
  • Plank - 3 sets of 1 minute
  • Crunches - 3 sets of 20 reps

Day 4 - Upper Body Strength

  • Pull-ups - 3 sets of 8 reps
  • Overhead Press - 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Tricep Dips - 3 sets of 15 reps

Day 5 - Lower Body Power

  • Bulgarian Split Squats - 3 sets of 12 reps per leg
  • Romanian Deadlifts - 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Step-Ups - 3 sets of 15 reps per leg

Day 6 - Cardio & Mobility

  • Cycling - 45 minutes
  • Yoga - 30 minutes
  • Foam Rolling - 15 minutes

Day 7 - Rest & Recovery

  • Stretching - 20 minutes
  • Light Walk - 30 minutes
  • Meditation - 10 minutes
